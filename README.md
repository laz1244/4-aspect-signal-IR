Used to control a 4 aspect railway signal with an arduino and ir sensor.

PIN OUT
1 - N/A
2 - RED indicator LED
3 - YELLOW indicator LED
4 - PRE YELLLOW indicator LED
5 - GREEN indicator LED
6 - N/A
7 - N/A
8 - Relay RED
9 - Relay YELLOW
10 - Relay GREEN
11 - Relay PREYELLOW
12 - IR sensor
13 - Button


Remote control:

1 - Red
4 - caution
7 - Precaution 
(*) - Green
3 - Flashing Caution
6 - Flashing Precaution
9 - Cyles through 
0 - “Party Mode”
(#) - Aspects off
